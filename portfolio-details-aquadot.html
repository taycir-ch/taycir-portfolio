Portfolio Details

Project Overview & Product Challenges

Aquadot is a centralized sports management platform designed to modernize the operational workflows of swimming clubs through a role-based digital ecosystem. The platform enables administrators, coaches, swimmers, and parents to interact through dedicated interfaces while improving coordination, communication, and performance tracking.

Traditional swimming club management often relies on fragmented tools, spreadsheets, and manual communication methods, creating inefficiencies across training organization, competition management, and athlete monitoring. Aquadot was designed to centralize these processes into a structured and scalable environment.

Multi-Role Platform Architecture

The platform architecture follows a role-based access model where each user type accesses a personalized workspace and functionalities adapted to their responsibilities. Administrative modules handle operational management, while athlete and parent interfaces provide visibility into schedules, performance metrics, and club activities.

Core Features

• Multi-role authentication and secure account access

• Administrative management of users and club operations

• Competition planning and season management

• Athlete profile and performance tracking

• Training schedules and attendance management

• Parent dashboard for monitoring swimmer activities

• Category and qualification management system

• Personalized dashboards for each user role

Product Impact

By consolidating administrative processes and athlete monitoring tools into a single platform, Aquadot reduces operational complexity while improving collaboration between coaches, swimmers, and parents. The platform enhances transparency and provides a structured environment for managing sports activities and performance data.
