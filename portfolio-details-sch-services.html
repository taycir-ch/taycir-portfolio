SCH Services — Logistics Management SaaS Platform

Project Overview & Engineering Challenges

SCH Services is a logistics management platform designed to simplify communication between customers and logistics service providers through a modern and responsive digital experience.

The platform centralizes logistics information, service requests, shipment estimations, and customer interactions into a unified system. The objective was to replace fragmented manual processes with a structured and scalable workflow capable of improving operational efficiency.

Platform Architecture & Workflow Design

The system architecture was designed around reusable frontend components and service-based backend APIs. Responsive interfaces ensure accessibility across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices while maintaining performance and usability consistency.

Core Features

  • Responsive logistics website
  • Shipment estimation requests
  • Contact and inquiry management
  • Service catalog management
  • Administrative dashboard
  • Multi-device optimized interfaces
  • Form validation and workflow handling

Engineering Impact

The platform transformed manual communication processes into structured digital workflows, improving customer interactions while providing an easier management experience for administrators.
